Good to know

Getting here & what to expect

Free parking

There's free off-street parking on site, so you can leave the car and settle in. Just over 3 miles from Aylesbury and Wendover.

Dogs & families welcome

Bring the whole family, four-legged members included. Dogs are welcome in the bar and garden, with water bowls always on hand.

Walkers' rest stop

Right by the reservoir and a short hop from Coombe Hill and Wendover Woods, the perfect place to refuel after a ramble.
