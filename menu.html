Something for everyone

As a Charles Wells house we take our beer seriously. You'll find Eagle IPA on at all times, alongside rotating guest ales kept in tip-top condition. Not a beer drinker? There's a well-chosen wine list, classic cocktails, ciders and plenty of soft options too.

  • Real Ales

    Eagle IPA plus changing guest ales from Charles Wells & beyond.

  • Wine

    A thoughtfully chosen list of reds, whites, rosé and fizz by the glass or bottle.

  • Cocktails & Spirits

    Proper classics and a good range of gins, served just how you like them.

  • Cider & Soft Drinks

    Crisp ciders, lagers and non-alcoholic choices for all.
